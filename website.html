Grand Theft Auto III (also referred to as GTA III or GTA 3) is an open-world action-adventure game which was developed by Rockstar North (previously known as DMA Design). It was originally released in 2001 for the PlayStation 2 but it was eventaully released on other platfroms such as Xbox and PC later on. It is the third main game of the Grand Theft Auto series, and it is the fifth overall installment.

Grand Theft Auto III is set in the fictional crime-filled "Liberty City". The protagonist goes by the name "Claude". The game is set in the timeline 2001. The game follows the adventures of Claude in the crime-filled city. Calude is a criminal who was betrayed by his girlfriend Catalina during a bankheist. After this betrayal Claude begins to work with other criminals which range from individuals to big factions within the city such as the Mafia and Yakuza.

Grand Theft Auto III is set in an open-world environment. It is the first GTA gane which is in a fully 3D, moving away from the 2D top-down perspective of all other GTA games before GTA III. It was developed by DMA Design and it is the final release of DMA Design before they renamed to Rockstar North.

On 11 November, 2021, the game was re-released for its 20-year anniversary, under the title "Grand Theft Auto III - Definative Edition". The "Definitive Edition" was included in the "Grand Theft Auto: The Trilogy" in which there were 2 other Grand Theft Auto games. In the "Definitive Edition" the graphics were improved so the game looks much better graphics wise.


Storyline

Grand Theft Auto III is an action-adventure video game developed by Rockstar Games. The game was released in 2001 and is set in the fictional Liberty City, a sprawling metropolis inspired by New York City. The story follows the protagonist, Claude, who is betrayed by his girlfriend Catalina during a bank heist. Catalina shoots Claude and leaves him for dead.

However, Claude survives and is arrested by the police. While being transported to prison, his prison transport is attacked by Colombian Cartel members, and Claude escapes. With no loyalty left to anyone, Claude embarks on a journey in Liberty City, working for various criminal organizations and individuals to climb the criminal ladder.

As Claude progresses through the criminal underworld, he becomes involved with various factions, including the Italian Mafia, Yakuza, and corrupt police officers. The overarching goal is to find and exact revenge on those who betrayed him.

The story is filled with betrayal, crime, and a web of interconnected characters. Along the way, Claude encounters a variety of eccentric characters, including corrupt politicians, mob bosses, and quirky side characters. The narrative unfolds through a series of missions, each contributing to the overall storyline.

GTA III is notable for its open-world design, allowing players to explore Liberty City freely and undertake missions at their own pace. The game received critical acclaim for its innovative gameplay, engaging story, and the introduction of a fully 3D open-world environment.

City

The game takes place in Liberty City, which is loosely based on some real life cities of the USA east coast, including New York City, Philadelphia, Detroit, Baltimore, Chicago and Pittsburgh, but it is not one of them in particular. There are different districts in Liberty City. It consists of three islands - Portland, Staunton Island, and Shoreside Vale. The three islands are very similar to the real life islands of Brooklyn, Manhattan, and Staten Island.

Portland: This is the initial area where players begin their journey. It includes locations like the Red Light District, St. Mark's, and Portland Harbor. Portland serves as the introductory district, introducing players to various criminal factions and activities.

Staunton Island: As players progress in the game, they gain access to Staunton Island, which offers a more upscale and business-oriented environment. Key locations include Belleville Park, Newport, and the elevated train system connecting different parts of the island.

Shoreside Vale: Unlocked later in the game, Shoreside Vale is the third and final district. It features residential areas, a dam, and the Francis International Airport. Shoreside Vale becomes a significant setting for the concluding parts of the storyline.
